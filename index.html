Our Mission

Revolutionizing Urban Sanitation


EcoSense is a Final Year Engineering Project designed to tackle the global waste management crisis. Traditional methods are inefficient, costly, and environmentally damaging.

By deploying a network of smart sensors and connecting them to a cloud-based dashboard, we provide municipalities with the data they need to make informed decisions, reduce operational costs, and improve the quality of life for citizens.

Process

How It Works


1

Sensors Detect

IoT devices inside bins measure waste volume using ultrasonic waves and transmit data over GSM/Wi-Fi.

2

Cloud Processing

Node.js server receives data, validates it, and stores it in MongoDB. Analytics are generated in real-time.

3

Decision Making

Admins view the dashboard. If bins hit 80%, the system triggers an alert and suggests a truck route.
